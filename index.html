ლექტორები

მარლენ სულამანიძე

სკოლის დამაარსებელი

Specialist on plastic, reconstructive and aesthetic surgery. Marlen was born in Georgia in 1947. In 1972 he graduated from the Medical University in Irkutsk. From 1974 to 1984 his specialization was maxillofacial surgery and later he started specializing in plastic and aesthetic surgery first in Georgia and from 1993 in Moscow. Since that time he has made over 20 thousand surgical operations, including over 5 thousand on nose shape deformity and 7 thousand on Aptos methods.
Dr. Marlen Sulamanidze is a member of Society of plastic, reconstructive and aesthetic surgeons of Russia, Georgia (GeoPRAS), ASPS (American Society of Plastic Surgeons), IPRAS, an honorary member of National Society of aesthetic surgeons of France, an honorary president of Japanese society of liposuction, a member of American society of dermatosurgeons. He has published over 70 scientific works, 18 of them in the leading international journals.
Dr. Sulamanidze has 16 patents and priorities of invention on plastic surgery, including 4 international, such as Aptos Threads, Wire Scalpel, a new implant for aesthetic operations, remedial bra, mammoplasty method, dermotension method. Also he has worked out a great number of original operations on his specialty.

Training and certification in the specialty “Cosmetology” in 2014 at the Russian University of Friendship of Peoples (RUDN).
Is an active member of:

– Society of Plastic Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgeons of Georgia (GeoPRAS)

Performed more than 8 thousand operations, and more than 400 training courses on methods of Aptos in more than 50 countries.Has published over 50 scientific papers, including the leading international journals. Anti – Aging Beauty Trophy 2015 – Winner of the Best Clinical Case.

გიორგი სულამანიძე

M.D. PhD Plastic Surgeon, Dermatologist.

A specialist in aesthetic surgery and aesthetic dermatology was born in 1979 in Georgia. In 2002 graduated from the Medical State University of Russia (Moscow, Russia). From 2002 till 2007 graduated in Master's Degree Program and PhD Degree Program in Plastic Surgery in the Russian Scientific Surgery Center’s (RAMS, Moscow, Russia) in Department of Plastic, Reconstructive Microsurgery, and get PhD degree on the theme New methods of liposuction. In 2015 graduated in Master’s Degree Program in Dermatology in RUDN University. Member of International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ISAPS) since 2013. From 2003 member of American Society of Dermatologic Surgery (ASDS). From 2011 member of International Confederation for Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgery (IPRAS). From March 2003 is honorary member of a French Society of Aesthetic Surgery. From September 2001 is honorary member of Japanese Liposuction Society. A member of Society of Plastic, Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgeons in Russia and Georgia (SPRAS, GeoPRAS). From 2011 member of Aesthetic Medicine Society of Russia.

In 2008 awarded Gold Scalpel in nomination Plastic Surgeon of The Year of Russia.

Published more than 65 scientific articles in leading international issues.

Obtained 18 patents and priorities of invention in plastic surgery, 8 of which are international. Among them are patents for: Aptos threads, Wire Scalpel, a new implant for aesthetic operations, therapeutic brassiere, and mammaplasty and dermothensy methods. Devised a variety of new original operations in his profession, including methods of contour compression in rhinoplasty, horizontal mammaplasty methods, methods of “subcutaneous brassiere”, methods of accelerated expanding, wire and liquid-gelular dermothensy, methods of minimally invasive surgery to eliminate discernible manifestation of aging process (wrinkles, ptosis of soft tissues), and other aesthetic deformations.
